Tanganyika

72" x 36"
Acrylic and charcoal on canvas
Tanganyika is a long lake in central Africa. People who live around the lake mostly speak Swahili, and the name means something about "sailing wide." The picture is a scene of a mother and daughter sitting on a living room couch listening to the man of the house. The background is a flat but vast wilderness of the lake.
